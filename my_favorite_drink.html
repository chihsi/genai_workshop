珍珠奶茶的起源

Origin of Bubble Milk Tea

珍珠奶茶的起源是春水堂。

Bubble Milk Tea originated from Chun Shui Tang.

在歷代飲茶歷史中即有將茶加料的先例，在創辦人劉漢介先生對於茶的專研及領導下，請春水堂研發團隊發揮各式各樣的創意。在1986年的某次測試下，決定結合童年記憶，史無前例將台灣庶民小吃「粉圓」加入香醇奶茶，1987年正式推出銷售，命名曰「珍珠奶茶」，打破點心與茶飲的界線。

Throughout tea history, there have been precedents for adding ingredients to tea. Under the leadership and tea expertise of founder Mr. Liu Han-Chieh, Chun Shui Tang's R&D team unleashed their creativity. In a 1986 experiment, they decided to combine childhood memories by adding Taiwan's popular snack "tapioca pearls" to rich milk tea. In 1987, this innovative drink was officially launched and named "Bubble Milk Tea," breaking the boundary between snacks and tea beverages.

平均一年可售出約 100 萬餘杯，成為享譽世界的代表性飲料—台灣之光。

On average, over one million cups are sold each year, making it a world-renowned iconic drink—truly the pride of Taiwan.
